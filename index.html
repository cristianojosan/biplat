About Us

Our Specialization

Our company specializes in sheet metal bending and the manufacturing of custom metal elements of any complexity.

Materials

A wide range of materials is used in our production, including aluminium, galvanized steel, stainless steel, and polymer-coated steel.

Technical Specifications

  • Metal thickness: up to 1.5 mm
  • Product length: up to 3.2 m

Our Facilities

We operate our own large production facility as well as a logistics hub for storing raw materials and finished products.

Our Production Capabilities

  • We work with our own or client-supplied materials, with storage available until installation.
  • Detailed reports on produced elements and material consumption are provided.
  • Full control over the production process from raw material to finished product.

Pricing & Flexible Cooperation

Trusted by Industry Leaders

For over five years, we have been delivering complex solutions for Microsoft and Amazon data centres in Sweden, including:

  • Installation of sandwich panels
  • Rain-screen façade systems
  • Flashings and standing seam roofing

We understand the strict requirements for technical specifications and project deadlines, especially when it comes to urgent requests and fast-track production.

Expanding Across Europe
